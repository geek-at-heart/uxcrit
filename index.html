Frequently Asked Questions

Are you redesigning my whole product?

No. It takes a long time to ramp up in a new business. We spend our time evaluating the usability, interactions, and workflows of your app. We won't make up new features, or redesign your logo or branding. You can choose whether we focus broadly on a general health audit, or dive deep into redesigning a specific, critical workflow (with visual examples).

What do you need from me?

Let us know if you want a general audit or a deep-dive into a specific workflow. If the latter, send us a list of 1-3 views or pages that need the most attention. If your app is behind a login, just create a test account with pre-populated data that we'll work against.

Who is this for?

SaaS founders, e-commerce store owners, and small to medium-sized businesses without dedicated UX talent who need clear, actionable recommendations.
